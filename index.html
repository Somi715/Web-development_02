About INIP

INIP (Places In India) is your ultimate guide to exploring the magnificent diversity of India. From the snow-capped Himalayas to the tropical beaches of Kerala, from the technological hubs of Bangalore to the spiritual centers of Varanasi, India offers an incredible array of destinations.

Our platform connects travelers with the most popular tourist attractions across all 28 Indian states and 8 union territories. Whether you're seeking adventure, culture, nature, or spirituality, INIP helps you discover the perfect destination for your journey.

Featured Experiences: Visit world-famous monuments like the Taj Mahal, experience the backwaters of Kerala, trek in the Himalayas, explore ancient temples, enjoy wildlife safaris, and immerse yourself in India's rich cultural heritage.

Each state page provides detailed information about the most visited and hidden gem destinations, complete with descriptions, locations, and travel tips to make your Indian adventure unforgettable.
